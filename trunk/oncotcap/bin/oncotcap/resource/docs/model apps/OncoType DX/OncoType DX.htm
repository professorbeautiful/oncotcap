OncoType DX model

This is a model of limited scope, designed to be combined with other models for broader applications. It combines the expression levels of the 21 genes comprising the OncoType DX prognostic test for node-tamoxifen-treated breast cancer (NEJM 351;27 December 30, 2004).

Gene expression levels are currently taken to be random. We hope to obtain patient-level original data expression levels, to allow sampling from a bootstrap model or smoothed model.

The output is the recurrence score, with risk group classification. the output appears in the top-most panel of the SinglePatient output window.
