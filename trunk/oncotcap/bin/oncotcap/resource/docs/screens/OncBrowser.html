The OncBrowser

The start-up window for OncoTCap is the OncBrowser, a master console for authoring, manipulating, and viewing all manner of items related to knowledge acquisition and modeling.

It has a menu & button bar, and one or two OncBrowser panels. The menu bar is self-explanatory. To show or hide the top or the bottom OncBrowser, tou can click on the lilttle black triangles .

Each OncBrowser panel has three sections:

  • The Tri-flow Controller (upper left)
  • The Browser Tree (right)
  • The Filter (lower left)
